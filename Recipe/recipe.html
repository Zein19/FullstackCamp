Lentils recipe

Ingredients (for 6 people)

  • 3 Cups of Lentils
  • 9 Cups of Water
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 Can of Fish/other meat of choosing

Procedure

  1. Add cups of water and lentils to a boiling pot and boil on full heat for 20 minutes.
  2. In another pan, drizzle oil on full heat and add your chopped onion until it caramelises.
  3. Add chili seasoning and other spices, and then add diced tomatoes to the pan.
  4. Add your fish/meat to the mix.
  5. Drain the lentils and now add to a pot with your mixture.
  6. Leave it to simmer until it reaches preffered consistency

Enjoy!
